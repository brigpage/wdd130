Overview

Purpose

Hello All! Welcome to River Gwen Rafting (RGR). Here we conquer our fears of the great white rapids! It is not for the faint of heart through. Not only is there adrenaline pulsing danger at every twist and turn, the river is also teeming with wildlife eagerly waiting for a quick snack though. These trials do not come without reward though. Whatever comes within arm’s length is free game for the taking! Here at RGR we strive for a family building experience. Along with taking on the rapids, each night comes with relationship building activities and scenic campsites to bring your group closer to one another. Sign up now! THE RIVER AWAITS!

Audience

Here at River Gwen Rafting (RGR) we welcome individuals of all walks of life to build and develop the relationships in their lives. - Are you a family man just trying to find a fun activity away from civilization to better connect to your roots? - Are you an overburdened mother looking for a way to feel alive again and do something daring? - Are you a struggle adolescent who is looking for a chance to prove to others that you CAN be independent in a stressful environment? Then RGR is going to feel like a true home away from home! Here at RGR we leave technology behind us, striving to live off the land and learn basic survival skills critical to a free mind and body. RGR offers a variety of trips and adventures, conducive to which ever level of daring you wish to do. Prove the nay sayers wrong and show them you are still exciting. Those that go through RGR recognize the level of independence and freedom a trip down the river provides. If you can conquer the rapids, you can conquer the foes in your life, whether those are physical, emotional, or mental. You CAN do it!
